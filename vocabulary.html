好词好句

从修改过程中提炼的数据描述高级词汇和优秀表达，按表达类型分类

illustrate 趋势描述
▶

释义：illustrate（图示、说明）— 比 show 更正式，是 Task 1 高分范文中最常用的题目改述动词。

上下文：The line graph illustrates the average carbon dioxide (CO2) emitted per person in four European countries...

exhibited 趋势描述
▶

释义：exhibited（展现、表现出）— 比 showed 更学术正式，是描述数据趋势的高级动词。

上下文：Sweden also exhibited a downward trend after reaching a peak in 1977.

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions

declined gradually 趋势描述
▶

释义：declined gradually（逐渐下降）— declined 比 decreased 更正式，动词+副词的语序更自然。

上下文：...although its figure declined gradually.

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions

rose considerably 趋势描述
▶

释义：rose considerably（大幅上升）— rose 比 went up 更正式，considerably 比 significantly 在非统计语境下更准确。

上下文：Portugal also rose considerably, from about 1 tonne to just above 5 tonnes in 2007.

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions

approximately 数据引用
▶

释义：approximately（大约）— 比 about 更正式，是学术写作中引用数据的标准用词。

上下文：...at approximately 11 metric tonnes per person.

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions

recorded significantly lower figures 数据引用
▶

释义：使用数据描述专用动词 recorded + 名词 figures，比 "were much lower" 更正式。

上下文：...Italy and Portugal recorded significantly lower figures, at about 4 tonnes and 1 tonne respectively.

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions

reaching its peak 数据引用
▶

释义：现在分词短语，表示"达到峰值"。比 which was its highest point 定语从句更简洁，peak 是描述最高点的标准用词。

上下文：Sweden rose to just over 10 tonnes, reaching its peak, while...

A decade later 时间表达
▶

释义：A decade later（十年后）— 比 Ten years later 更简练正式，是时间跨度描述的高级表达。

上下文：A decade later, Sweden rose to just over 10 tonnes...

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions

throughout the entire period 时间表达
▶

释义：throughout the entire period（在整个时期内）— 比 during the whole period 更正式地道，是概述段常用表达。

上下文：...the UK produced the highest amount of CO2 throughout the entire period...

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions

over the 40-year period 时间表达
▶

释义：明确写出时间跨度，增加信息量，比泛泛的 over the period 更具体。

上下文：...Italy increased steadily from 4 to nearly 8 tonnes over the 40-year period.

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions

gradually 程度/幅度
▶

释义：gradually（逐渐地）— 比 slowly 更正式，是描述缓慢变化趋势的标准副词。

上下文：The UK also dropped, but more gradually, finishing at about 8.5 tonnes.

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions

with + 名词 + 现在分词（独立主格结构） 概述句式
▶

句式：...experienced steady increases, with Italy becoming the second largest emitter by the end of the period.

说明："with + 名词 + 分词" 结构可以在不增加新从句的情况下补充信息，使句子更紧凑、更高级。适合在 Overview 段末尾补充细节。

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions

between...and 改述时间范围 概述句式
▶

句式：The line graph illustrates ... between 1967 and 2007.

说明：用 between...and 替换题目中的 from...to，是最基本但必须掌握的改述技巧，避免照抄原题被扣分。

来源：2026-03-16 CO2 Emissions
